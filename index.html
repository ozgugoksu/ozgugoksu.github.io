Ozgu Goksu

My name is Ozgu Goksu (Original : Özgü Göksu how to pronounce?). I am a first year PhD student at University of Glasgow, and my supervisor is Dr. Nicolas Pugeault, and member of CVAS group.

Before my PhD, I did master at Gebze Technical University with Prof. Dr. Erchan Aptoula, and have an undergraduate degree from Gebze Technical University in Computer Science and Engineering. During my master, I worked as research assistant for 3 years at the same university, and worked as project member at my supervisor's TUBITAK (The Scientific and Technological Research Council of Turkey) project.

Research

My current research interests are in the areas of computer vision, machine learning and deep learning, specically self-supervised learning. The subject of my master thesis is content-based image retrieval via deep learning, and I worked with remote sesning datasets such as multispectral, hyperspectral and 3-channels images.
